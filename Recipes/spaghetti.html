Spaghetti Recipe

Description

For the following recipe, the spaghetti dish contains beautiful fresh tomatoes used for the sauce, bring brightness to the dish, as well as freshly made pasta with gorgeous basil garnish

The recipe can be used to make the dish within an astounding 45 minutes and is extremely simple and great for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Fresh tomatoes
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Flour to make pasta
  • basil

Instructions

  1. First crush the tomatoes in a heated pan
  2. Then season the sauce with salt and pepper to your liking
  3. Add some basil to the sauce and let it simmer on low
  4. Now make the pasta by using a pasta roller/cutter machine
  5. Boil some water and drop the pasta until cooked
  6. Drop the pasta into the sauce and serve with basil garnish!
