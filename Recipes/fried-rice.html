Fried Rice Recipe

Description

For the following recipe, the rice dish contains beautiful freshly snatched eggs, crazy seasoning blend, in combination with freshly cooked rice

The recipe can be used to make the dish within an astounding 20 minutes and is extremely simple and great for the whole family!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Salt and Pepper
  • MSG
  • Fresh eggs

Instructions

  1. First cook some rice
  2. Then heat up and oil the wok
  3. Add the eggs in and scramble them
  4. Now add the rice and seasonings in especially the MSG
  5. Ensure that the fried rice is constantly stirred
  6. Serve the dish!
